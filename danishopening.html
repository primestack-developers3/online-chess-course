Danish Gambit

Sharp, aggressive gambit play after 1.e4 e5 2.d4 exd4 3.c3

Description

The Danish Gambit (1.e4 e5 2.d4 exd4 3.c3) is a sharp, aggressive gambit where White sacrifices pawns for rapid development and attacking chances. It's risky but can lead to explosive games if Black is unprepared.
