French Defense

Solid, strategic response to 1.e4

Description

The French Defense (1.e4 e6) is a solid, strategic response to 1.e4. Black aims to challenge White’s center with ...d5. The structure often leads to closed positions where Black seeks queenside counterplay and central breaks with ...c5 or ...f6. The opening emphasizes pawn-structure and piece coordination — a favorite among positional players.
