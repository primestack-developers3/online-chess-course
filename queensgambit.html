Queen's Gambit

Classical centre play after 1.d4 d5 2.c4

Description

The Queen’s Gambit (1.d4 d5 2.c4) is a classical opening that challenges Black’s center. If accepted, White gains central control; if declined, positional tension builds. It’s solid and offers both strategic depth and dynamic potential.
