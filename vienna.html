Vienna Game

Flexible, tactical and transpositional play after 1.e4 e5 2.Nc3

Description

The Vienna Game (1.e4 e5 2.Nc3) is a flexible opening that can transpose into gambits or more positional lines. It often leads to rich tactical opportunities, especially if White plays f4 early, aiming for rapid central control and kingside attacks.
