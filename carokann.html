Caro-Kann Defense

Solid, resilient play after 1.e4 c6

Description

The Caro-Kann Defense (1.e4 c6) is a solid and resilient opening choice. By playing ...d5 early, Black challenges White’s center without creating major pawn weaknesses. It often leads to slightly passive but very durable positions, suited to strategic, positional players.
