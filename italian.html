Italian Game

Quick development, center control and early pressure on f7

Description

The Italian Game begins 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4 and emphasizes quick development, central control and early pressure on Black's f7 square. It can lead to quiet positional play (Giuoco Piano) or sharp tactics (Two Knights Defense). Typical plans include c3 and d4, rapid castling and coordinated piece play. It’s an instructive opening for all levels.
