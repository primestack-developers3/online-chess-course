Trading Pieces

Simplify, relieve pressure, and steer the game

Description

Trading pieces means exchanging material, typically of equal value. It’s a strategic tool to relieve pressure, simplify into a favorable endgame, or neutralize threats. Strong players use trades to transform the position into one that suits their strengths.
