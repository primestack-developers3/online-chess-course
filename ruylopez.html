Ruy Lopez

Classical, strategic responses to 1.e4 e5

Description

The Ruy Lopez (1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5) is a classical and deeply analyzed opening. White pressures the e5 pawn and prepares for long-term positional advantages. It offers rich strategic complexity and is popular at all levels.
