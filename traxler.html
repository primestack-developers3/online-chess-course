Traxler

Sharply tactical counterattacks after 1.e4 e5

Description

The Traxler Counterattack (Wilkes-Barre Variation) begins with 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4 Nf6 4.Ng5 Bc5. Instead of defending, Black launches an aggressive counterattack. It leads to chaotic, tactical positions where precise calculation is critical. It's ideal for fearless players who thrive on complications.
