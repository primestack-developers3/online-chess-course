Arabian Knight

Classic rook + knight mating pattern

Description

The Arabian Mate is a classic checkmate pattern using a rook and knight. The knight controls escape squares while the rook delivers mate, usually trapping the king on the corner. It's simple, elegant, and useful in practical play.
