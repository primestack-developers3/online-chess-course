Topic Of The Month


Grand Master Hikaru Nakamora won the comet open chess tournament!

Hikaru Nakamura triumphed in the 2025 Comet Open, showcasing his trademark brilliance in online rapid chess. The event, hosted by Chess.com and open to all titled players using the Comet browser, featured intense knockout matches with a 3+0 time format. Nakamura remained unbeaten throughout six rounds, defeating elite players like Nihal Sarin and Parham Maghsoodloo in tense tiebreaks before conquering Denis Lazavik 2.5–1.5 in the Grand Final. His composure under pressure and exceptional endgame precision stood out,reaffirming why he’s one of the greatest online competitors. This victory earned him $45,000 and added another milestone to his already decorated career. Beyond the result, the Comet Open highlighted the growing significance of browser-based tournaments in modern chess. For fans and aspiring players, Nakamura’s win serves as proof that consistency, adaptability, and mental toughness continue to define champions—even in the fast-paced world of online chess.
