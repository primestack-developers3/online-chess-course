Sicilian Defense

Description


The Sicilian Defense (1.e4 c5) is one of the most aggressive and complex responses to
1.e4. It creates an asymmetrical position that offers Black dynamic counterplay, particularly on the queenside, while White usually attacks on the kingside.
There are many sub-variations like the Najdorf, Dragon, and Sveshnikov, each with its own sharp and theoretical character.
It is a favorite at all levels due to its winning chances and fighting nature.
