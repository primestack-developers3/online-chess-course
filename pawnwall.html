Pawn Wall

Solid pawn structures and central blockades

Description

Pawn Wall refers to a solid pawn structure—often c3–d4–e3 or c4–d3–e4—that creates a central blockade. It offers stability, restricts piece activity, and is used in closed systems such as the London or Stonewall.
