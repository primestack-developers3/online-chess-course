Fried Liver

Sharp tactical attack from the Italian

Description

The Fried Liver Attack arises after 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4 Nf6 4.Ng5 d5 5.exd5 Nxd5 6.Nxf7. It’s a sharp tactical line where White sacrifices a knight for a quick attack on the exposed Black king. Effective in faster time controls but risky if Black defends accurately.
